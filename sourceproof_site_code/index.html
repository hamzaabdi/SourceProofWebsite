Why Somaliland Requires Independent Verification

Live livestock exports move fast, involve many parties, and small issues become expensive late-stage failures. Our role is simple: provide neutral, time-stamped evidence before payment and before loading.

Decision support, not opinions

Buyers use this verification to reduce disputes, document due diligence, and confirm export readiness before committing funds.

Corridor-dependent sourcing

Gulf buyers rely on narrow lanes. If one shipment fails, timelines compress and recovery options shrink.

Late-stage failures are costly

Readiness issues often show up days before loading. Verification shifts discovery earlier—before money moves.

No neutral eyes on the ground

Without independent checks, buyers depend on intermediaries with incentives to hide problems.

This is why serious buyers verify before payment — not after problems appear.
